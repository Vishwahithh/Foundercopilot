## Key Findings — Week 1

### ✓ Hypothesis Validation

**Core finding:** All 5 founders have a clear bottleneck between input generation (calls, videos, docs) and output publishing (posts, tweets, emails).

- **Marc Lou:** Daily tweets + YouTube videos (likely manual repurposing from calls)
- **Guillaume Moubeche:** $20M ARR but sporadic LinkedIn output (content extraction friction)
- **Adam Robinson:** High-engagement LinkedIn posts (but likely hand-crafted, 30–60 min each)
- **Simon Høiberg:** High-volume TikTok/Shorts but likely manual clipping from long-form
- **Jonathan Rintala:** Cross-platform posting (LinkedIn + TikTok) but separate workflows

**Implication for Founder Copilot:** This is a **real, measurable problem** across all revenue stages and channels. Early adopters likely: Marc Lou, Simon Høiberg, Jonathan Rintala (highest output volume).

---

### 📊 Messaging Angle Preferences

Each founder has a signature style:

| Founder | Primary Angle | Secondary | Output Style |
|---------|---------------|-----------|--------------|
| Marc Lou | Process (how-to) | Story | Daily tweets + YouTube |
| Guillaume Moubeche | Results (metrics) | Story | Weekly high-touch videos |
| Adam Robinson | Playbook (structure) | Problem | Weekly LinkedIn posts |
| Simon Høiberg | Systems (automation) | How-to | Daily short-form |
| Jonathan Rintala | Growth (content strategy) | Benefit | 3–5x/week cross-platform |

**Implication:** Founder Copilot should learn founder preferences and rank angle suggestions accordingly.

### 🎯 Early Adoption Signals

**Highest Likelihood:**
1. **Adam Robinson** — Clear repurposing bottleneck (sales calls → LinkedIn playbooks)

3. **Simon Høiberg** — High-volume output (would appreciate efficiency gain)

**Lower Priority:**
